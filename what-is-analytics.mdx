**Twinalyze Analytics** is an end-to-end product analytics platform powered by **lightweight, developer-friendly SDKs**. It helps you track user behavior in real time—so you can clearly understand how people use your app and what to improve next.

Track everything from **screen views, tab switches, fragment navigation, session flow, notifications engagement**, to **ad events and revenue**—all without SDK bloat.

---

## What You Can Track

- **App Navigation**  
  Activity views, fragment views, tab switches, back screens, drawer open/close, deep visible fragment tracking.

- **Sessions & Engagement**  
  Session starts/ends, foreground/background, splash time, time spent, user journeys.

- **Notifications & Campaigns**  
  Scheduled, instant, or draft campaigns (multi-language supported). Track delivered, opened, dismissed, and engagement in real time.

- **Ad Analytics**  
  Requests, impressions, clicks, failures, and **revenue events** across multiple ad networks.

- **Custom Events (Auto + Manual)**  
  Auto-track common events, and also send your own custom events for full flexibility.
